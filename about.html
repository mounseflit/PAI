About PocketAI

Making AI Accessible to Everyone

We are democratizing artificial intelligence through on-device intelligence, bringing powerful AI to every pocket regardless of connectivity or location.

Our Mission

PocketAI was founded with a singular vision: bringing AI to every pocket. Today, 2.6 billion people lack reliable internet access, effectively excluding them from the AI revolution. We believe that intelligence should not be gated by connectivity. By running advanced language models directly on mobile devices, we are building a future where anyone, anywhere, can harness the power of AI — privately, instantly, and without compromise. Our mission is to close the digital divide and ensure that the benefits of artificial intelligence reach developing nations and underserved communities worldwide.

Our Values

What drives everything we build

Privacy First

Your data never leaves your device. We engineer every feature with zero-knowledge architecture, ensuring true privacy by design.

Accessibility

AI for the next billion users. We optimize for low-end devices and offline environments so no one is left behind in the AI revolution.

Innovation

We push the boundaries of what is possible on mobile hardware, pioneering novel quantization and inference techniques for edge AI.

Open Collaboration

We work with researchers, universities, and the open-source community to advance on-device AI for the benefit of everyone.
