FAQ

Frequently asked questions

PocketAI is a mobile application that runs advanced AI language models directly on your smartphone. Unlike traditional AI assistants that send your data to remote servers, PocketAI processes everything locally on your device, giving you powerful AI capabilities with complete privacy and full offline functionality.
PocketAI uses optimized and quantized versions of large language models that have been specifically engineered to run on mobile hardware. We leverage your phone's GPU and NPU (Neural Processing Unit) to perform inference locally. The AI model is downloaded once during setup and then runs entirely on your device without needing an internet connection.
Yes, absolutely. With PocketAI, your conversations, documents, and personal data never leave your device. There are no servers logging your queries, no third-party data sharing, and no cloud processing of your private information. Your data stays on your phone at all times. When hybrid mode is enabled for complex tasks, you explicitly choose what data, if any, is sent to the cloud.
PocketAI is designed to run on a wide range of iOS and Android devices. We support most smartphones released in the past 3-4 years with a minimum of 4GB RAM. Our team has tested the app on over 1,200 different device configurations to ensure broad compatibility and optimized performance across different chipsets including Qualcomm Snapdragon, Apple A-series/M-series, MediaTek Dimensity, and Samsung Exynos.
PocketAI offers a free Starter plan that includes basic on-device models, an offline AI assistant, and privacy-first design with community support. Our Premium plan at $10/month unlocks advanced models, hybrid multi-agent AI, voice mode, cloud sync, and priority support. For enterprise and business needs, we offer custom pricing with SDK and API access, dedicated SLAs, and on-premise deployment options.
