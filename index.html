Features

Everything you need from an AI assistant — without the cloud

100% Private

Your data never leaves your device. No servers, no logs, no third-party access. True privacy, not just a promise.

Offline-First

Full AI capabilities without internet. Fly, commute, or live off-grid — PocketAI works wherever you are, always.

Lightning Fast

No network round-trips. Responses are instant, running natively on your phone's GPU and NPU with optimized inference.

Hybrid Intelligence

Simple tasks run locally. Complex tasks route to the cloud when you allow it. You decide the balance.

Multi-Agent System

Coordinate multiple AI agents on your device for complex workflows — research, writing, scheduling, all at once.

Fully Personalized

Your model adapts to you over time. Writing style, preferences, workflows — all learned locally, stored locally.
