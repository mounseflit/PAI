Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information to site owners. Cookies can be "persistent" (remaining on your device until they expire or are deleted) or "session-based" (deleted when you close your browser).

This Cookie Policy explains how PocketAI Technologies ("PocketAI," "we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website at pai-technology.com and related web services (the "Website"). This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which provides further details on how we handle your personal data.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies on our Website for several purposes:

  • Essential Operations: To enable core website functionality, such as maintaining your session when you log in, processing secure transactions, and remembering your cookie consent preferences.
  • Performance & Analytics: To understand how visitors interact with our Website, which pages are visited most frequently, and to identify areas for improvement. This data is collected in an aggregated, anonymized form.
  • User Preferences: To remember choices you make (such as language preference, theme settings, or region) to provide a more personalized experience on subsequent visits.
PocketAI Mobile App: The PocketAI mobile application uses minimal cookies and tracking technologies due to its on-device processing architecture. The app does not use third-party advertising cookies. Local storage on your device is managed entirely by you and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly opt in to cloud features.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We take a minimal approach to third-party cookies. Our Website may include the following third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Stripe (Payment Processing): When you visit our pricing or checkout pages, Stripe may set cookies necessary for secure payment processing and fraud prevention. These are essential cookies governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Embedded Content: If you interact with embedded content (such as video tutorials or documentation widgets), those third-party services may set their own cookies. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

We do not use any third-party advertising or marketing cookies. We do not participate in cross-site tracking networks, and we do not allow any third party to use cookies on our Website for advertising purposes.

5. Managing Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Cookie Consent Banner: When you first visit our Website, you can choose which categories of cookies to accept or decline through our consent banner. You can update your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.
  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically configure your browser to block or delete cookies. Consult your browser's help documentation for specific instructions:
    • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
    • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
    • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
    • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions
  • Do Not Track: Our Website respects the "Do Not Track" (DNT) browser signal. When we detect a DNT signal, we disable all non-essential cookies and analytics tracking.

Please note that disabling essential cookies may impair certain functionality of the Website, such as the ability to log in or maintain preferences across pages. Disabling analytics or preference cookies will not affect core Website functionality.

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in the cookies we use, our practices, or applicable legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Post the updated policy on our Website with a revised "Last Updated" date
  • Display a new cookie consent banner so you can review and update your preferences
  • Notify registered users via email if changes significantly affect the way we process data

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the Website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Cookie Policy.
